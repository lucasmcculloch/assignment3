Past/History

I was born and raised in the picturesque city of Lethbridge, Alberta, nestled amidst the rolling prairies and framed by the majestic peaks of the Rocky Mountains. Growing up in this tight-knit community, I was surrounded by a supportive network of family and friends who instilled in me values of hard work, perseverance, and community spirit. From an early age, I developed a passion for soccer, spending countless hours honing my skills on the local fields. The camaraderie of the sport and the thrill of competition fueled my ambition, setting the stage for the journey that lay ahead.
